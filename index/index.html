About me

My friends have described me as a man of many talents, a serial hobbiest, and a polymath. Some of my past and present interests include: Trail running, cycling, and fitness, I have experience as a NCAA D1 athlete and as a coach. Music (as a creator) I am learning to play the Harp, I dabble in ukelele, theory, keyboards, synthesizers, and rapping. Music (as a consumer) I listen to a wide range, of electronic, videogame music, hip-hop, progressive house, reggae, classical and North Indiana classical. My favorites acts are Daft Punk, Migos, and Bach. I know some things about finance, web design, logic, chess, old video games, government & politics, astrophysics & cosmology, wood working, electronic engineering, evolutionary biology, rubiks cubes, zoology, campuing/outdoors, table tennis, coffee, pro sports, mycology, skateboarding, anime/manga and philosophy. I am always learning and getting into new things and revisitng old.
